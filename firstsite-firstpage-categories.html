How, though?

Starting to write can prove just as difficult as any other creative hobby. You need a place to start!

As many would advise: it's better to just start the first project you're thinking about than to keep on worrying about whether it'll be interesting or not. The first few will be rather haphazard regarding meaning but that's okay. What's important is to not linger on any one project too long. You'll never be fully satisfied about any of your work so it's better to accept that and move on once a piece is finished.

It's important to value quantity at first so you can get a feel for what it is like to write, no matter what style or structure you're looking to adopt.
